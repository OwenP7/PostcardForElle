Hello from Turkey!

Proud is an understatement, I hope you know the weight and sincerity behind these words. I’ll be watching on May 1st, regardless of anything else. Did I tell you I’m missing my own graduation? Maybe I send these somewhat selfishly to help me grow, but in the theme of past statements, I don’t see how more communication of my thoughts could hurt a situation caused by a lack thereof.

Best of luck with finals and enjoy your last week of school!!!
